    SIGNS OF DRUG ADDICTION

    The signs of drug addiction aren’t always obvious. Substances such as painkillers or other prescription medications may fly under the radar because individuals receive those substances from doctors. However, any addiction to drugs is a dangerous one. Every drug produces different physical and mental symptoms. However, most drugs will cause some form of withdrawal. When a person doesn’t use the substance for a period of time, they will experience uncomfortable, flu-like symptoms as a response. These symptoms will drive them to use the drug again in order to feel relief. In addition to withdrawal symptoms and cravings, individuals with drug addictions will also exhibit several behavioral signs. Some of these signs include: • Withdrawal from previously enjoyed activities and hobbies • Straining relationships with loved ones • Poor performance at work or school • Sudden financial problems or asking for money A combination of these signs may indicate the need for the best drug addiction treatment center California can offer.
