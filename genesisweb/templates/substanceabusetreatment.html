    COMFORT DETOX

    For most people in a substance abuse treatment program, chemical detox is the most uncomfortable part of the recovery process. The withdrawal symptoms can at times be daunting, or downright painful. Our Comfort Detox program provides our guests with the greatest amount of relief from symptoms. Our team of doctors and licensed addiction professionals will tend to your every need and discomfort during the detoxification process. Detoxification can be a dangerous process if attempted outside the supervision of a licensed medical professional. Our Comfort Detox clients have around-the-clock medical monitoring and supervision.     RESIDENTIAL DRUG AND ALCOHOL TREATMENT PROGRAMS

    We do addiction treatment differently. We have no single process that everyone goes through, nor do we herd you like cattle from one 12-step meeting to the next. Each individual is thoroughly assessed upon arrival and together we design a unique treatment program to achieve your goals and expectations for recovery. From initial assessment to discharge, Pillars Recovery professionals utilize a variety of treatment methods which may include the following: • Clinically managed Comfort Detox • 30-60-90 day residential drug and alcohol treatment programs • Psychiatric care for co-occurring disorders • Specialized holistic chronic pain management protocols • LGBTQ affirmative addiction treatment • Individual therapy sessions • Group therapy sessions • Marriage and family counseling • Acupuncture and massage therapy • Art therapy sessions • Recreational activities • Aftercare and transitional planning The minimum length of stay at Pillars Recovery is 30 days, however we recommend a stay as long as possible to help ensure your success in recovery. During your initial and subsequent assessments, we’ll discuss your need for treatment and recommend a length of stay that works for you. If you have questions about payment or insurance, we have a variety of resources available to us to get you the help you need. More information is available on the Insurance page.


    TRIPLE-DIAGNOSIS™ CHRONIC PAIN MANAGEMENT & TREATMENT

    Chronic pain and prescription addiction have long been a challenge in traditional substance abuse treatment centers. Removing the pain management medications often means that detox is more uncomfortable and pain may become unmanageable during the process. To combat this, we’ve created a Triple-Diagnosis™ treatment program to work around the unique needs of people who suffer from long-term chronic pain. We utilize a variety of holistic and medical treatment methods to combat chronic pain while learning how to live independent of often harmful prescription pain medication. Our focus through our Triple-Diagnosis™ program is truly to holistically treat the disease of addiction through mind-body-spirit healing.


    AFTERCARE & FAMILY PROGRAMS

    In addition to our detox and substance abuse treatment programs, we offer a variety of resources to you to help you maintain your sobriety once you leave primary treatment. Our Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP) is a solid way to continue treatment while living at home or in sober living. It offers more freedom than residential treatment, while still providing support to those new to recovery. Our Intensive Outpatient Programs (IOP) are a great way to ease back into normal life. You may return to your normal daily schedule of work or school while attending IOP during the hours most convenient for you. At any point during your stay at Genesis Sober, we offer marriage and family therapy as well as group sessions to help you heal the relational wounds caused by addiction.
