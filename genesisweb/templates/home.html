    THE GENESIS SOBER EXPERIENCE


    At Genesis Sober, we believe in high quality personalized care and a strong sense of community that can elevate a young person’s self-esteem and renew his/her desire to live. We offer specialized mental health treatment services for both gender above 18 years of age. Our primary focus is on drugs addiction and mental health challenges such as crisis, trauma, loss and developmental difficulties. Genesis sober Community clients receive at least one individual psychotherapy session each week for the entire duration of treatment provided by licensed clinicians with years of experience. Our emphasis is on individual growth and resocialization facilitated by community involvement through dynamic group experiences, family therapy, and experiential learning activities.


    ABOUT GENESIS SOBER


    At Genesis Sober Community, we strive to not only assist people to free themselves from alcohol & other drugs but also to ensure that they embark on a holistic goal directed behavior change with the main objective of transforming the individuals to responsible persons in the society.
    Our programs are tailored to ensure that our residents become self sustaining through personal empowerment activities. We believe that with the serene & supportive environment offered, our organization is able to set in motion critical processes that will eventually herald our clients’ optimal actualization of inner potentials.




    GENESIS SOBER PROGRAMS


    From our medically-managed Comfort Detox to our after care programs, Genesis Sober offers a full continuum of care to help you overcome your addiction. When you enter our facility, our team determines which programs will work best for you. Whether you need a Triple Diagnosis™ program or a Partial Hospitalization Program, Genesis Sober is ready to guide you in every step of the way toward recovery.




    WORD FROM THE DIRECTOR


    Drug addiction is a chronic relapsing and fatal disease whose devastating effects have far and wide ramifications that dehumanize, degrade, and debilitate its victims and their significant others in immeasurable ways. Restoring persons with addictions back to normal functionalism is a huge task that demands honest and committed efforts by those of us who aspire to do so. It calls on us to delve deeper into the core of our being for passions that push us beyond the call of duty as we attend to such passions. To ensure that our patients benefit optimally from our services, we work with others in the field to ensure an all round approach that includes medical detoxification, residential treatment, family therapy, after care , and formidable support systems. We believe that it is for this well rounded approach to treatment that has realized the greater majority of our patients’ achieve recovery culminating in reintegration back to their families, communities, and the wider society.
