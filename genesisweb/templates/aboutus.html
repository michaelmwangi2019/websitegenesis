About Us

“Genesis Sober was created to be a refuge for recovery in a world stricken by addiction.”

Genesis Sober is a refuge in a world stricken by addiction. For young men and women, addiction can completely derail their lives and make it difficult to move forward. When you learn about Genesis Sober, you’ll also discover a facility that’s ready to guide young adults on a journey toward a happier and healthier life.


ABOUT GENESIS SOBER

Genesis Sober provides residential drug and alcohol recovery programs including detox and treatment, as well as outpatient aftercare programs. Our homes are located in comfortable and serene settings on the beautiful hills of Kiambu County. A key feature about Genesis Sober is that we offer services for both men and women. Our men’s and women's residential treatment center is located in Githunguri Springs. Our mission is to help everyone who desires to become free from the chains of addiction. As the owners and staff of Genesis Sober, we were once slaves to our addictions ourselves. We have all found the freedom that comes with long-term, successful recovery. Therefore, we want to show you how you can find that freedom too. When you learn about Genesis Sober, you’ll understand that our vision is to see a positive impact in the lives of the people who entrust us with their recovery. It is an honor for us to be of service in guiding others to happy, successful lives in long-term recovery.

OUR HISTORY

We created Genesis Sober to help people who want and need recovery. We believe that the cost of treatment should not be a barrier to accessing treatment. Furthermore, we were fed up with turning away people who desperately wanted to find help, but were unable to because they couldn’t afford it. We do not put a price on human life. An aspect about Genesis Sober that sets us apart from other facilities is our location. We created our treatment centers in luxurious Kiambu County highland homes because we believe that comfort is essential to recovery. Furthermore, these serene settings remove all distractions from recovery and offer our clients a “sun-kissed” Kirigiti lifestyle. Additionally, our compassionate and caring staff provides experienced, state-of-the-art, and proven treatment methods. Our desire is to see every one of our guests come to experience all of the promises that successful, long-term recovery has to offer.


FULLY LICENSED AND ACCREDITED DRUG AND ALCOHOL DETOX TREATMENT

We are fully licensed and accredited by the State of Kiambu and the Joint Commission. Our caring and compassionate clinical and support staff will make your stay comfortable and also help ensure your successful long-term recovery. When you come to Genesis Sober, you’re joining a family that will encourage and support you throughout your journey.

BEGIN YOUR JOURNEY

At Genesis Sober, we’re here to guide you on a journey to healing from addiction. Our caring and compassionate counselors are ready to work with you to reach your goals. Furthermore, we treat people, not just disease. We’re experts at helping you find and heal from the traumas that facilitate addiction and relapse. Some of the programs we offer include the following:

  • Comfort Detox
  • Triple Diagnosis™
  • Dual diagnosis treatment
  • Intensive outpatient program
  • Partial hospitalization program
  • Substance abuse treatment


When you’re ready to begin your recovery journey, reach out to us. We’re available 24/7 to answer any questions you have about your recovery. Call today at 0723-865-762 to learn more about Genesis Sober and how we can help you achieve a fulfilling and successful life.
